Vision

WEFEA builds effective, globally-accessible software and optimizes secure network connectivity to offer variety of services, allowing financial information (or other sensitive details) to be exchanged safely and confidentially. Our uniquely-built digital ecosystems will benefit an array of diverse businesses, and our model is expanding from a local to a global market.

Company

WEFEA offers effective software and secure connectivity for Fin-Tech businesses and their transactions.

Specifically, WEFEA currently offers three major products

  1. Credit card payment gateway
  2. Student Loan Repayment (SLR-67 Program)
  3. Trade Execution for the stock market

To maintain a growth strategy, the WEFEA business model is to buy and build. In the credit card business and trade execution of the stock market, WEFEA is in the process of acquiring two companies. In the student loan repayment business, WEFEA is designing a new product that will assist borrowers in repaying their student loans faster and at a reduced cost.
