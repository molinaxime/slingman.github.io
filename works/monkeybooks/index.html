Books for Birthdays!

Present a book to the Chinese Immersion School at De Avila Library in honor of the birthday child!
The Birthday Book program is a perfect way to celebrate a child's birthday and help fill our school library with great books! Share with grandparents, aunts/uncles, or others who want to do something special to recognize your child and the importance of reading. It is also a great way to recognize a teacher's birthday!

How to Order?

  1. Order a preselected book from the Chinese Immersion School at De Avila wishlist(s).
  2. On the shopping cart page, click on "enter birthday book program details" and fill out the child's full name and then complete checkout.
  3. When the book arrives, a special book plate will honor the child.
